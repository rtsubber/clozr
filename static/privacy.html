Questions We Get From Compliance-Conscious Buyers

Can Clozr actually see my transcripts if I self-host? ▼
No. When you self-host, transcript processing happens on your server using your own API keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, or local models like Ollama). We have no access to the content. Zero. If you use our cloud version, transcripts are processed and then deleted per our retention policy — they are never stored permanently or used for training.
What AI model processes my transcripts? ▼
Your choice. Self-hosted users can connect their own OpenAI key, use Anthropic Claude, or run a fully local model with no internet connection required. Cloud users use our API pipeline with dedicated (not shared) processing and immediate deletion post-generation.
Is Clozr HIPAA compliant? ▼
We are HIPAA-conscious by design, not HIPAA-certified as a blanket claim. For healthcare consultants, self-hosting is the correct configuration — your data never leaves your environment. We recommend pairing Clozr with a BAA from your cloud provider (AWS, Azure, GCP all offer these). We're happy to walk through your specific setup with your compliance team.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼
Cloud plan: all data deleted within 30 days of cancellation. You can request immediate deletion. Self-hosted: you own the server, you delete it yourself — we have nothing to delete.
Does Clozr notify other meeting participants that I'm recording? ▼
Clozr doesn't — but you are legally responsible for compliance with recording consent laws in your jurisdiction (many states and countries require it). We include a consent reminder in the app before recording starts. We also provide a sample disclosure script in our Legal Resources section. This isn't us dodging responsibility — it's us being honest that this is your professional obligation and we want to help you meet it.
Can my whole team use it without everyone getting auto-enrolled in email lists? ▼
Yes. Team accounts work exactly as you'd expect: you invite who you invite. Nobody gets a surprise email from Clozr. Nobody's contact is harvested. We don't market to your team members unless they individually opt in.
